\begin{tabular}{|p{0.2\textwidth}|p{0.3\textwidth}|p{0.16\textwidth}|p{0.08\textwidth}|}

\hline
Heap-Increase-Key(A,i,k) & Increases key at index i to new value k & $O(\log n)$ & $O(1)$ \\
